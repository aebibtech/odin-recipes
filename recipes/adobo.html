Adobo

Back to Recipe List

Description

Ingredients

  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Bay Leaves
  • Black Peppercorns (Paminta)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Beef Broth
  • Meat of choice (Chicken, Pork, Fish)

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a cooking pot.
  2. Add the garlic.
  3. Add the peppercorns and bay leaves
  4. Put the meat in the cooking pot.
  5. Pour the soy sauce and beef broth (or water).
  6. Pour-in the vinegar.
  7. Taste your adobo and decide to add salt if needed.
  8. Transfer to a serving plate.
